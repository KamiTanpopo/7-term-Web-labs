Dog breeds really do have distinct personalities

The genetic and behavioral data come from different sets of dogs, the work cannot link a breed's specific behavioral tendencies to any one gene. "This paper doesn't call out any particular breed for its behavior. It relies on behaviors that are found in many breeds," says Heidi Parker.

The findings suggest behavior is guided by the same genes in many species.
